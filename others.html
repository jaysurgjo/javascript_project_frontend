    About

    Do you ever feel like something is missing in your life? Whether you need a confidence boost to send that first Tinder message on a hot Summer day, or you're looking to dull the pain of your 8th divorce in the middle of January, WHAT AM I DRINKING? is here to assist you. Featuring the ability to create your own drink cards and pull past user entries, WAID is here for you when you need inspiration the most.
